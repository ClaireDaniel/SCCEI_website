Vision Statement

Connecting and empowering emerging innovators to deliver world class leadership for Smart Cities.

Purpose

SCCEI is a platform for emerging innovators in smart cities to meet, share, learn and promote how technology can be used to make the environment more liveable.

About

SCCEI was lauched at the end of 2017 in respose to approaches by young professionals keen to get involved with the work of Smart Cities Council. The group has been set up with the support of the wider Smart Cities Council and is self-managed by its founding members. In 2018 SCCEI is looking to get new members on board and establish a regular program of events.
